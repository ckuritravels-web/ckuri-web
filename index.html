4‑Day Signature Itinerary

Day 1

Valle de la Luna + Valle del Arcoíris

Iconic dunes and mineral colors
  • Morning: Rainbow Valley + Yerbas Buenas petroglyphs
  • Afternoon: Valle de la Luna viewpoints and short walks
  • Sunset at a scenic overlook
Includes: Private transport, certified guide, entries, water & premium snacks
Day 2

Laguna Cejar + Laguna Chaxa + Toconao

Floating waters, flamingos and a desert village
  • Float in Laguna Cejar (high salinity)
  • Wildlife at Chaxa in the Atacama Salt Flat
  • Cultural walk through Toconao: plaza, church, crafts
Includes: Private transport, guide, entries, towels, water & snacks
Day 3

Geysers del Tatio + Termas

Dawn geothermal fields and hot springs
  • Early departure to see Tatio at sunrise
  • Short guided walk across the geyser field
  • Relaxing hot springs on the way back
Includes: Private transport, guide, entries, breakfast & water
Day 4

Ruta de los Salares

High‑Andean lagoons and wide landscapes
  • Scenic 4x4 across lesser‑visited salt flats
  • Fauna spotting (flamingos, vicuñas, foxes)
  • Boutique picnic in nature
Includes: Private transport, guide, entries, picnic & water
